Your Running Mindset

Avrey Low

Something that I watch when I am running is what I am telling myself...am I telling myself I am too slow? That there's no way I can go one more step? Although it may seem strange, running is more than just a physical sport...there is a mental aspect to it. If you tell yourself that you can't do it, you're not going to be able to do it, even if you have the physical ability to do so. Of course, there are still limitations with the physical aspect, but if you don't believe in yourself then you will not perform as well as you could if you believe in your abilities.
