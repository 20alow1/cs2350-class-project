What does it mean?

Your BMI is a measure of whether your body weight is healthy for a person of your height. It is not a perfect assessment of your health, but it is a good starting point. Here are the categories:

< 18.5 = Underweight

18.5 - 24.9 = Normal weight

25 - 29.9 = Overweight

30+ = Obese
